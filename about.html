Who we are

How we work

Our process

Simple, transparent, no surprises. Here's what working with Vuka Digital looks like from first message to launch.

01

You reach out

Fill in our contact form or drop us a WhatsApp. Tell us what your business does and what you're looking to build. No forms to fill out, no calls to schedule first — just a conversation.

02

We scope it together

We'll ask the right questions to understand your goals, your audience, and your budget. Then we'll recommend the right package or solution — honestly, even if it means a cheaper option.

03

You approve, we start

Once you're happy with the scope and price, a 50% deposit kicks off the project. We share a brief and get to work — no disappearing acts, regular updates throughout.

04

You review, we refine

When the first version is ready, we share it for review. Your revision rounds are there to make it exactly right — not to nickel-and-dime you on changes.

05

We launch and stay in your corner

Final payment, final files, and we push it live. Then — if you want — we stay on with a monthly care plan to keep everything running smoothly.
