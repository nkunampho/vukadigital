Common questions

Before you ask

Do I need to buy hosting separately?

No. Static sites can be hosted free on Netlify or Vercel. If you need a domain, we'll guide you through buying one — a .co.za domain is around R50/year.

How do I pay?

We accept EFT bank transfer. A 50% deposit is required to start, with the balance due on delivery.

What do I need to provide?

Your business name, what you do, and any content (text, photos, logo) you already have. We'll handle the rest and fill gaps where needed.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes. Many clients start with Vuka Basic and upgrade to Pro once they see results. We'll credit what you've already paid toward the new package.

Still not sure which fits?

Send us a message. We'll recommend the right package for your business — honestly.
